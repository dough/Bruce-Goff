The work

The first day I arrived in Houston, to live with my Aunt Betty and Uncle Ralph, she took me to the apartment next door to see some art being exhibited in the apartment of Herb and Mary Greene, and the apartment next to this occupied by Bob Bowlby and Dick Whittaker. Art to me had beem some dusty Old Masters and architecture some old buildings.

I was astonished to find that I did not have to know anything to enjoy these paintings and could just look and say nothing. Then they began showing me drawings of a proposed development called Circle Towers and a round apartment building, and it actually made sense. They talked about the man behind the paintings and the building design and he made sense.
